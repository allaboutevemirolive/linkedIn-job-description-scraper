As a Mac Support Subject Matter Expert, you will be a key part of the team, tasked with improving the end user experience for Apple devices within the business.

Roles And Responsibilities

 * Provides expertise in the specification of AIA’s business requirements, performance levels and the scoping of Mac/JAMF relates initiatives, changes and service requests, ensuring traceability back to desire business outcomes.
 * The successful applicant will be a Mac, Apple & Jamf subject matter expert, providing customer service focused technical Mac, Apple & Jamf support to a large sized user base.
 * Assess vulnerability & patch information from compliance scans for Mac OS (any release) and determine application and production impacts.
 * Utilize enterprise tools, such as Jamf, to automate patch deployment and software deployment.
 * Play a key role in defining hardware standards, engineering and securing the standard desktop platform, implementation of policies and procedures to ensure consistency with customer goals, industry best practices, and regulatory requirements.
 * Provide Mac OS X knowledge and Engineering level experience for Mac OS X imaging and application packaging.
 * Exposure to hybrid Mac/PC environments, with experience supporting MacBook Air, MacBook Pro, iMac, Mac Pro, iPhone, and iPad.
 * Create and maintain base images for desktop and laptop computers including the automated portion of the overall imaging process.
 * Assists in proactively developing patch and vulnerability management procedures and processes within the operations team and in conjunction with business and IT partners.
 * Someone with expert knowledge of macOS, iOS & Windows 10 management and deployment as well as expert knowledge of scripting (AppleScript, Shell, Bash, PowerShell etc) for automation
 * Create and maintain detailed documentation, processes and procedures.
 * Actively participate in operational ITIL processes i.e. Incident, Change Management (CAB), Release Management, Problem Management, and Availability Management.
   
   

Minimum Job Requirements

 * Must have a minimum of 7 to 9 years’ experience with Apple Mac hardware and administering/supporting Mac OS X in a medium to large enterprise.
 * Expert knowledge in Jamf Pro - including system design, implementation and management.
 * Expert knowledge of macOS, iOS & Windows 10 management and deployment.
 * Expert knowledge of scripting (AppleScript, Shell, Bash, PowerShell etc) for automation.
 * Demonstrable experience in software packaging and deployment.
 * Knowledge of MDM technologies, preferably supporting Jamf running alongside Intune.
 * Knowledgeable in supporting Microsoft Office 365 and Azure Conditional Access.
 * Strong problem-solving experience to effectively troubleshoot/diagnose complex issues.
 * Applicable certifications to demonstrate technical competencies, for example, Apple Certified Support Professional (ACSP), Apple Certified Mac Technician (ACMT).
 * High proficiency with centralized Mac OS X security, encryption, and policy management as well as Active Directory integration
 * Excellent communications and interpersonal and presentation skills.
 * Experience of working within a fast paced BAU environment.
 * Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Information Systems, or other related business, scientific, or technical discipline.
