The SEA Build and Test team is responsible for supporting the development of Dyson products by producing prototypes and performing verification & test activities related to both software and hardware for all product categories.




About The Role




Upon joining us in the internship programme, you’ll be assigned to various tasks from day one. You will be contributing towards the development of test methods and rigs, simulation and verification testing to providing support during Mass Production manufacturing, based on your individual skills and experiences




Some Of Your Tasks Could Include




 * Solving engineering challenges to create better, faster, more cost-effective solutions for Dyson’s unique products
 * Conceptualizing and Designing experiments and necessary rigs/fixtures for component and system characterisation and validation
 * Investigating, Developing and Building the test rigs/fixtures to meet test specifications
 * Testing and Assessing robustness of our rigs/fixtures designs to provide repeatable and reliable test results
 * Supporting the execution of test method activities across categories




Working with teams across Research, Design & Development (RDD) with broad range of disciplines, you will be comfortable learning from others, but also sharing your own knowledge, ideas and opinions. We will support you with a development framework designed to help you make the most of your internship.




About You

 * Studying towards a Bachelor's degree in Engineering or Science related disciplines
 * Able to commit full-time for at least 12 weeks
 * Familiar with programming languages such as Matlab, Labview, Python, Java or similar

 * Able to demonstrate how you have applied technical knowledge and engineering skills to solve problems
 * Have a creative approach to problem solving to ensure our machines are better
