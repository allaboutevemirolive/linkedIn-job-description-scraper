Job Description

At Keysight, we help companies address their toughest challenges with precision and confidence. We offer the most precise electronic measurement tools in the industry, with advanced software to match. Our employees leverage their insight and passion to revolutionize electronic measurement and make a difference in the world.

As a R&D Hardware Intern you will be responsible but not limited to the following:

 * Research on technologies and support the production line.
 * Continuous improvement and maintenance of the company's existing proprietary hardware systems.
 * To carry out hardware prototype testing and characterization.
 * Understanding test program and make modification if necessary to achieve test requirements and data collection.
 * Perform hardware prototype rework.
 * Conduct instrument performance analysis.
 * Perform circuit simulation to evaluate circuit performance.
   
   

Job Qualifications

Bachelor's Degree in any Engineering related fields.

Good Fundamental knowledge of electronic circuits especially in analog, digital logics and embedded system.

Passionate about circuit design, simulation and debugging.

Enthusiasm for analog circuit, power supply and control systems design and troubleshooting.
