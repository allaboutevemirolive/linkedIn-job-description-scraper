Key Responsibilities:




 * Perform failure analysis on electrical failing units post electrical test.
 * Support failure analysis on assembly failing units Familiar with failure analysis tools and techniques such as SEM, EDX, curve trace, TDR, hotspot analysis, X-ray, chemical decapsulation, cross-section
 * Prepare failure analysis reports upon the completion of the failure analysis
 * Able to read technical drawings and electrical circuit diagrams Maintenance of FA inspection tools
 * Able to lead and guide a group of FA technicians
 * Prepare monthly report to the management on the failure analysis lot status and findings
 * Able to work with external teams for issue identification
 * Good communication skills to explain the failure analysis findings and initiate discussions with the requestors




Qualifications:




 * Bachelor in Mechanical/ Mechatronics/ Electronics & Electrical Engineering
 * Min 5 years working experience performing failure analysis with focus on electrical failure analysis.
 * Computer literate
 * Familiar with FA tools such as curve tracer, hotspot analysis, TDR.
