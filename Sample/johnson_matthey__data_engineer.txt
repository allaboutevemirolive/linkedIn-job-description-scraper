Data Engineer

Vacancy: Data Engineer

Together for a cleaner, healthier world.

Johnson Matthey, a FTSE 100 company, is a global leader in sustainable technologies specialising in catalysis, precious metal products, chemicals and process technology. With operations in over 30 countries, we employ more than 13,000 people. Johnson Matthey uses science to make the world cleaner and healthier. Over the past two centuries we have built our reputation and place as a global leader through quality, integrity, and innovation. Today, more than 93% of the group's sales come from products and services which provide sustainability benefits through the positive impact they have on the environment, resource efficiency and human health, but that’s not enough. We have ambitious plans for growth and need talented individuals to help shape and lead us into our next century.

As a Data Platform Engineer you will be accountable for the end-to-end Agile Analytics / Digital Analytics Platform and technologies including Data Lake and Tableau front end reporting suite. They are responsible for the design and architecture of the JM BI, Digital and Analytics solutions, utilising business knowledge & technical expertise in the JM digital Analytics product sets (primarily Tableau, AWS & others).

Your responsibilities:

 * Enhance & support analytics platforms within JM primarily (but not exclusively) built out on the JM Data Platform / Data Lake built on AWS technologies
 * Become the JM “Product Owner” for the AWS Data Platform / Data Lake capability.
 * Provide input to the business to help support the definition of the overall sector based digital analytics roadmaps.
 * Work with vendors to manage both proofs of value and projects to deliver against defined business requirements and also scale up as necessary.
 * Provide definition of architecture standards – information, process, and technical for new technologies as adopted by JM
 * In conjunction with wider Data Office define governance procedures and processes including handover to support.
   
   

Requirements for the role:

 * Experience of working in Cloud Engineering/Technology (AWS / Azure / GCP)
 * You're an engineer at heart and enjoy the challenge of building elegant solutions
 * You want to work across a range of tech, trying out the latest tools and trends
 * You have a passion for automation, having previously worked with pipelines and used some scripting language(s)
 * You believe in lifelong learning, always looking for a 'better way'
 * You're happy to dive headfirst into anything that comes your way.
