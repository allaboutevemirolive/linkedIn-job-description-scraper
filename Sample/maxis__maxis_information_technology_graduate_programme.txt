Why does this job exist and why is it critical?




Overview/Your Journey

You will undergo comprehensive onboarding and training, including attachments at our retail and contact stores. These opportunities will provide you with hands-on experience and a deeper understanding of our business, all while keeping your learning journey simple and exciting.

 * To discover how Maxis uses the self-serve and mobile applications solutions to serve nationwide consumers and enterprise customers. You’ll learn about application architecture and design flow, as well as business critical functions to help you understand the advanced technology widely used in the telecommunications market OR
 * Immerse yourself in our Data Centre and Campus Network architecture, fundamental designs, perimeter security principals and design flows that will help to boost your knowledge in advance networking technology.
