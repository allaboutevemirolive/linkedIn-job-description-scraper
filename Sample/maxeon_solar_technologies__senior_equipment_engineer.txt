Summary Of Role

To work together with Process Engineer, Yield/Integration Engineer, and Equipment Technicians to optimize and maintain Oxidation equipment & automation to support safety, efficiency, and quality goals set by the company by way of continuous improvement.

Essential Duties And Responsibilities

 * To engage and work closely with Manufacturing Supervisors/Specialists, Process and
 * Equipment Technicians to resolve daily line issues and to sustain the lines.
 * To provide first level engineering support for Furnaces, Furnaces Abatement & Furnace Loader/Unloader Automation issues and be involved in
 * engineering projects such as cost reduction, OEE improvement, second sourcing, yield &
 * reliability improvement and new line/equipment start-up.
 * To review equipment data log, errors and SPC to ensure equipment are always running smoothly and develop necessary PM procedures, OCAPs and PM, etc.
 * To plan training programs for Equipment & PM Technicians for capability improvement.
 * To monitor and sustain daily equipment performance.
 * To maintain and achieve good mechanical and electrical yield.
 * To drive improvement projects to reduce repair and maintenance cost.
 * To perform root cause analysis on yield and address issues using problem solving methodologies such as 8D approach and 7QC Tools.
 * To support cost reduction for chemicals and spares using data analysis, engineering evaluation and characterizations.
 * To prepare for and attend required meetings.
 * To comply with the standards, norms and regulations of quality of the environment, health and safety and manufacturing.
 * To perform other tasks as required or assigned.
   

Related Experience & Educational Requirements

 * Degree or Master's Degree in the field of Mechanical, Electrical, Machatronics or related field.
 * Minimum 3 year experience in wafer cell manufacturing, wafer fabrication or PV Cells/
 * Module manufacturing background is preferable.
 * Fresh graduates with good CGPA are also encouraged to apply.
 * Analytical, able to perform risk analysis and mitigation with strong problem-solving skills
 * (FMEA, 5 whys, 8D).
 * Good command of computer knowledge including MS Windows applications.
 * Good team player with good communication and negotiation skills.
 * Ability to speak, write and read in Mandarin is an added advantage.
