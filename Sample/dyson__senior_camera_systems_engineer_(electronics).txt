Senior Camera Systems Engineer (Electronics)

About Us




Dyson is a global technology enterprise. We solve the problems others choose to ignore, with surprising new inventions that defy convention and simply work better. We’re growing fast and our ambition is huge - more categories, more locations and more people.




About The Role




The Senior Camera Systems Engineer will join the Robotics Hardware Integration team as a part of Dyson’s Hardware Electronics department. The candidate will be responsible for the system-level analysis, design, and development of advanced camera systems from concept to production for use across a wide range of exciting future products.




 * Design, build and test proof of concepts to evaluate camera systems hardware configuration.
 * Define the optical, electrical, and optoelectronic components’ specifications to meet camera systems requirements.
 * Review design proposals from camera integrator partners and provide professional feedback to drive design maturity.
 * Design and perform laboratory experiments to verify and validate the hardware design of camera systems.
 * Provide in-depth analysis and solutions for identified design issues during camera systems development.
 * Devise test methods and evaluate emerging camera systems technologies from image sensor and camera module vendors.


About you


 * A degree in a relevant discipline (e.g. Electronics Engineering, Mechatronics Engineering, Optical Engineering, Physics)
 * At least 2 years of experience working on the design, development, and verification of camera systems.
 * Expertise in digital camera and optics technology.
 * Good understanding of the interaction of key camera components.
 * Experience with designing/performing optical and electrical laboratory testing for camera systems.
 * Proficiency in at least one of the major schematic capture and layout packages such as Altium Designer and Siemens Xpedition.
 * Proficiency in data analysis, image/signal processing and automation of tests using Python, Matlab, LabVIEW or other programming languages.
 * Strong analytical skills with the ability to work independently and effectively as a team.
 * A highly motivated person with good communication skills and the ability to work within a team.
 * Independent, integrity and self-driven with a strong focus on results.
 * Possess a strong command of written and spoken English.
