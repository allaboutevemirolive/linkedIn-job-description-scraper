About The Role




We are looking for a Graduate Product Performance Engineer to join our Technology Development team, to support the key performance development of our new floorcare products.




Working as part of a small team you will be responsible for the primary product performance which included Pick up and Separation Systems. You will be working alongside the design team to create new ways to measure and predict key performance parameters; and ensure that our technologies are up to meeting their ambitious targets. You will use a mixture of test, simulation, and engineering judgement to solve the problems that arise as we develop our technologies and products. You will work from the very early stages of a project, taking responsibility of development when the technologies leave the research stage, developing them to a feasible solution and then supporting our SEA counterparts to take them through to production.




Some Of Your Tasks Could Include




 * Identify and manage product/sub-assembly/part level challenges associated with product performance and resolve them successfully.
 * Work side by side with the Design Manager and design engineering for allocated projects and provide technical consultancies and advice the dimension that critical to performance through system diagram.
 * Conduct experiments or other relevant analysis methods and tools to develop and validate our understanding and solutions.
 * Ability to identify risk and keep all stakeholders up to date on progress and status of allocated projects.
 * Confidently and clearly present and articulate ideas to audience in both written and verbal communication.
 * Drive analysis of data, ensure robust test methodology and conclusive proposals for timely delivery of solutions and verification report using basic competency in Matlab, Minitab, Excel and/or Python.
 * Build and maintain effective working relationships with design engineers and broader cross teams at all levels.
