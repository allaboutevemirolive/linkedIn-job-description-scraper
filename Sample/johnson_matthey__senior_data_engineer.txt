Senior Data Engineer

Vacancy: Senior Data Engineer

Location: Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia (Hybrid)

Job Family: IT

Together for a cleaner, healthier world.

Johnson Matthey, a FTSE 100 company, is a global leader in sustainable technologies specialising in catalysis, precious metal products, chemicals and process technology. With operations in over 30 countries, we employ more than 13,000 people. Johnson Matthey uses science to make the world cleaner and healthier. Over the past two centuries we have built our reputation and place as a global leader through quality, integrity, and innovation. Today, more than 93% of the group's sales come from products and services which provide sustainability benefits through the positive impact they have on the environment, resource efficiency and human health, but that’s not enough. We have ambitious plans for growth and need talented individuals to help shape and lead us into our next century.

The Senior Data Engineer fills the gap between raw data and data analysis by building the infrastructure and tools needed to transform, clean and load data into systems ready for investigation. Working the data office (onshore and Offshore), the Senior Data Engineer will focus on transforming unstructured data from all data sources into data that is Findable, Accessible, Interoperable and Reusable (FAIR)

Your responsibilities:

Lead with design / delivery of the JM data Lake / agile data warehouse asset as it is developed including:

 * Building data pipelines: Managed data pipelines consist of a series of stages through which data flows across a managed landscape.
 * Drive Automation through effective metadata management: use innovative and modern tools, techniques and architectures to automate the most-common, repeatable and tedious data preparation and integration tasks.
 * Be Familiar with and use modern data preparation, integration and AI-enabled metadata management tools and techniques.
 * Collaborate and lead along with The data office delivery team, Data Scientists and Business Analysts across JM in refining their requirements for data and analytics initiatives
 * Work closely in ensuring compliance and governance during data use to ensure that the data users and consumers use the data provisioned to them responsibly
 * Be a data and analytics evangelist: This role will promote the available data and analytics capabilities and expertise to the business and educate them in leveraging these capabilities in achieving their goals.
   
   

Requirements for the role:

 * Advanced Experience in building cloud-based data pipelines
 * Extensive Experience developing data storage solutions and pipelines in AWS or Azure
 * Extensive Experience in turning unstructured datasets into FAIR data
 * Undergraduate or Postgraduate degree in Data Science/Data Engineering/
 * Advanced Experience with KNIME, Tableau, PowerBI, Python
 * Advanced experience of working across AWS and Azure Cloud
