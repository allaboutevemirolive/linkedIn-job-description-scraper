Platform Convergence Lead

Job Description




As a Platform Convergence Lead you will be responsible for:




 * Builds, document, delivers and maintains E2E migration plan to move all clients to the new Discover Front end tool and to transition to a dis-aggregated data model, and understands all dependencies
 * Oversees and understands entire project, manages risks, and takes remedial action to address gaps
 * Owns the communication on the project status and ensures necessary KPIs are in place
 * Ensures project appropriately resourced across all areas
 * Facilitate strategic decision making and serves as escalation point
 * Works with enablement teams to properly analyze and identify gaps and areas for improvement in legacy production environments/tools and drive convergence to Discover and On Demand
 * Identify and implement solutions that translate into standardization and process efficiency while balancing local BAU need
 * Provide technical and process expertise to deployment of Discover, by directing and influencing; also assist remote located resources to implement projects in the most cost-effective way
 * Serve as a change agent to improve business processes and implement changes that “stick”; drive for continuous business process improvements
 * Maintain effective working relationships with BAU leaders (need support of BAU team members)
 * Operate in a virtual/multi-cultural environment, liaising as needed with stakeholders and colleagues
 * Facilitate training plans for local teams in Pre-transition/ transition phase (outside NOC)
 * Working in partnership with Moonshot PMO team, GOT team and the local country team, defines, and continually monitors the execution of operational elements of a project plan, identifying and recommending alternative workable solutions to deliver the overall project to schedule




Qualifications




 * Project management skills
 * Bachelor’s degree in any relevant education background
 * Minimum 2 years’ experience in PM (Data research/BPO/Market research)
 * Good level of E2E understanding of our operational process
 * Leadership skills. Not in terms of people management, but in terms of leading a x-functional, x-country teams in this program and to manage stakeholders in the program
 * Change management skills
 * Ability to handle ambiguous environment
 * Able to handle stressful circumstances
 * Excellent leadership, communication (written, verbal and presentation) and interpersonal skills
 * Excellent communication skill and presentation skill
 * Good problem solving skill
