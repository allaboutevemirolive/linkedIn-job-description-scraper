About The Role
 * Provide on-going support for the SAP ECC system in relation to programming work in ABAP.
 * Ensure programs are coded and delivered according to AIA programming standards while maintaining necessary documentation of the development work
 * Ensure SAP PO interface configuration are maintained with due diligence, taking into consideration on impact to all existing SAP PO interfaces
 * Ensure all addition/changes to SAP PO interfaces and configuration are documented and saved according to AIA practice
 * Attending to incidents & changes requests for SAP(primarily focused in moderate to high complexity based on Complexity Matrix defined), primarily in the areas of ABAP Workflow, SAPScript, Smartforms, IDocs and PO.
 * Develop & maintain development documentation (Technical specifications) and other relevant documentation as necessary to ensure sustainability of development work and compliance to Change Governance.
 * Deploy changes related to incidents & change requests to production system.
 * Perform POC and feasibility studies for proposed solution in SAP
 * Perform research beyond SAP solution and integration between SAP and other solution
 * The resolution time of incidents & change requests must meet agreed SLA time based on incident/change request’s priority.
 * In term of ABAP specific skillset, he or she is expected to be able to perform ABAP programming in various areas listed but not limited to list below:
    * Forms: SAPscript, Smartforms, PDF forms
    * Report: Query report, List reporting, ALV reporting,
    * Interface: Flat file (in txt/excel format), IDOC, ALE, BAPI, Data Medium Exchange Engine (DMEE)
    * Conversion: BDC, LSMW
    * Enhancement: user exits, BADI, Enhancement Implementation, Enhancement Spot, Business Transaction Events (BTE).
    * Others: Workflow, Screen painting, Dialog/DYNPRO programming
    * Any other new SAP technology in the future.

 * In the event when assigned to SAP projects, he or she is expected to perform ABAP programming during such assignment with minimal guidance, and adhere to AIA project management process and practice.
 * Conduct technical training where required on system interfaces
 * Perform ABAP code review of ABAP colleagues (QA check) to ensure programs delivered adhere to AIA programming standards
 * Demonstrate keen eye for identifying business process improvements opportunities and to highlight to the team
