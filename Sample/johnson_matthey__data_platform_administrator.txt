As a Data Platform Administrator you will be primarily responsible for working alongside the data platform engineer to ensure that JM data platform continues to work and ensuring JM is getting the most from its data platform assets ensuring good architecture is in place. The role will be responsible for ensuring that appropriate licences are provided to business users for Tableau, KNIME, Intelligens and other data software used by JM, it will ensure that our data analytics servers operate to their most optimum by monitoring their performance and carrying out “health checks”. The role is also responsible for working with the software vendors to ensure JM is utilising the most from its data software.

Your responsibilities:

 * Manage the use of a number applications such as KNIME, Collibra, SciBite and Power BI
 * Work with the Platform Engineer to ensure that the data platform is optimized by understanding its architecture.
 * Develop and maintain procedures for system maintenance (upgrade/backup/restore)
 * Monitor the performance of applications and operating model, and perform trouble shooting
 * Manage users and user group
   
   

Requirements for the role:

 * Experience of managing Servers and P1 incidents related to server crash.
 * Basic experience of platform architecture
 * Knowledge of data structures and database fundamentals and cloud platform fundamentals (AWS, Azure)
 * Knowledge of TCP/IP protocols, understanding security groups.
 * Experience with Linux and server upgrades.
 * Experience as Power BI admin.
 * Minimum 1 year experience in a similar role.
