About The Role




Responsible to perform and execute Shariah research & advisory function on the business, activities and operations carried out by the Company.




Job Responsibilities




 * Perform Shariah Advisory and Research function as required by Shariah Governance policy document of Bank Negara Malaysia to ensure business units’ compliance with Shariah.
 * Perform research and provide advices on product, operational or distribution matters and issues that require Shariah solutions.
 * Conduct review on product and non-product documents such operational forms, agency circular or campaign.
 * Conduct review or update Shariah and operational policies, guidelines / SOP / manual.
 * Act as Shariah subject matter expert lead in providing support or liaising with the Shariah control functions (Shariah review, Shariah risk and Shariah audit).
 * Evaluate and provide Shariah input on risk events for the purpose of ORION KRI and Incident reporting (monthly and quarterly).
 * Evaluate and provide Shariah input on audit findings and issues.
 * Monitor the treatment of re-classified securities.
 * Support the escalation of Shariah non-compliance findings to the management, Shariah Committee, Board and Bank Negara and monitoring the implementation of the action plans as approved by the Shariah Committee.
 * Plan and execute Shariah training and awareness.
 * Develop new and refine existing training materials.
 * Provide support to APAC in developing training materials.
 * Support the implementation of the Structured Shariah Training (SST).
 * Inculcate Shariah awareness culture among staff and intermediaries.
 * Develop, execute and monitor the Shariah compliant self-assessment tool
 * Prepare and implement checklist based on the Shariah rulings issued by the Shariah Advisory Council of Bank Negara and/or Shariah Committee.
 * Prepare and implement Shariah compliant checklist based on the Shariah standards issued by Bank Negara of any other relevant bodies.




Job Requirement




 * Degree in Shariah.
 * 5-7 years’ experience in handling Shariah research & advisory function
 * Proficient command of spoken and written English in order to effectively communicate with multiple stakeholders. Ability to communicate in Arabic is an added advantage.
 * Detail-oriented, efficient, responsible, self-motivated.
 * Positive thinking with growth mindset.
 * Preferably candidate with working experience in financial services (Takaful, Insurance or Banking).
