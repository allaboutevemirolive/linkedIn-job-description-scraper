Site Reliability Engineer- Workday

Where you fit in




As a Workday IT Engineer, you will be part of the Workday Functional DevOps team, supporting functional configuration that is an integral part of the Workday Security supporting various other modules in Workday. Also, you will be deeply involved in end-to-end system features implementation, from design, configuration, testing and defects resolution to post-deployment maintenance and troubleshooting. You will be required to understand both the technical and business side of HRIT processes, to deliver greater business impact and improved employee experience in a more affordable way.




What’s The Role




 * Actively contribute to value delivery through “One Team – One Goal”, “Self-Organizing” and “Continuous Improvement” agile principles. We operate with a strong emphasis on teamwork and close collaboration across all roles and functions
 * Troubleshoot issues in the Workday landscape & integrations to multiple systems – deploying fixes and/or working with other systems support teams to identify/deploy a fix
 * Monitor end-to-end Workday landscape using monitoring tools, investigate and pro-actively fix systemic issues either through system changes or working with other systems support teams
 * Ensuring the integrity of the end-to-end landscape, data flows and integrations with connected systems throughout the change and deployment lifecycle.
 * Have a solid understanding of user needs – capture/clarify requirements to enable impact analysis and delivery of committed change requests in Workday through sprints
 * Stay up to date with Workday Product development and Community, utilizing best practices and technical documentation available
 * Engage on a daily basis with colleagues from HRIT DevOps Team (Product Owner, Scrum Master, DevOps Analysts, DevOps Engineers, security, Information Risk Management (IRM) & other IT and HR areas specialists) and actively participate in scrum ceremonies




What We Need From You




 * Bachelor's Degree or equivalent work experience and minimum 5 years of experience in information technology
 * Quick learner who can upskill in Workday Security and Reporting module with our internal medium intense training. This also expects a Self-learning effort (under guidance)
 * Strong communication and interpersonal skills (verbal and listening skills)
 * Minimum of 3 years of Application development or support (any IT application)
 * Good to have Knowledge of ServiceNow and Azure tools
 * Ability to work in a team-oriented, collaborative environment, fast-paced and handle planned and unplanned responsibilities
 * Influencing and showcasing stakeholder management skills
