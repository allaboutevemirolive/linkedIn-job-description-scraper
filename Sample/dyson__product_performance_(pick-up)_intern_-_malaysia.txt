The Dyson Product Performance - Pickup team is responsible for delivering performance focused technologies that clean dust and debris from the floor. Marketing claims based on Pickup performance are key to Dyson’s success, so the role of Product Performance - Pickup is important for ensuring each technology is understood, robust and well controlled in production.




Product Performance - Pickup team works in RDD and contribute to the development of Dyson products from the evaluation of new technologies right up to verification of the final product. Product Performance - Pickup team will also manage system specifications, design, conduct development testing, monitor performance testing and provide solutions to further improve the machine’s performance.




About The Role




You will be assigned to the Product Performance – Pickup team for a 3-4 months internship. Aside of the standard training that Dyson provides, we will also be coaching you in Pickup Systems related technical knowledge to execute the project. The nature for the project will be related to new methodology development for the purpose of improving or revamp existing analysis strategies. Your tasks could include:




 * Optimize pickup technologies, with systematics and data driven experimental techniques
 * Utilise signal acquisition and analysis techniques to measure system performance and quantify improvements
 * To comprehend pickup critical parameters, their effects to other product performances and methods to verify them.
 * Manage and hands on for the investigative test
 * Interface with others team during improvement studies
 * Document the developed strategy in details for future knowledge sharing and improvement opportunities.
