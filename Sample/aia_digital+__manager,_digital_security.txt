About The Role

Oversee, develop, and manage Customer IAM service management processes to ensure that all services/products are delivered according to targets.

 * Oversee, develop, and manage Customer IAM service management processes to ensure that all services/products are delivered according to targets.
 * Manage vendors performance and to ensure that the vendors are delivering services and meeting service levels stated in the contract.
 * Manage and run service review with business leads, vendors and relevant stakeholders.
 * Manage and resolve customer feedbacks and escalations.
 * Ensure services/products are managed and delivered in accordance with company’s IT Security and other relevant policies and standard.
 * Contribute to short term and long-term strategic road maps for Customer IAM service management and ensure goals and initiatives are aligned with the Group Information Security objectives.
 * Translate business needs into service offering requirements.
 * Position the Customer IAM organization to embrace and support emerging customer service driven needs.
 * Collaborate with internal and external stakeholders to review existing processes, capture operational challenges, and develop business requirements for improvement.
 * To drive Continuous Service Improvement initiatives in improving service quality, efficiency, and compliance.
 * Oversee application onboarding according to security and UX best practices
 * Ensure daily operations and system workflows follow global standards and security policies.
 * Maintain relationship between group teams providing standardized requirements, technology development and security.
   
   

Job Requirements

Education

 * Degree in IT
   
   

Experience

 * 5-10 years of Information Security related working experience
 * 3-5 years of Service Management and Service/Operations improvement related working experience
 * Technical knowledge of IAM, Cybersecurity and other security domains. UX/CX
 * Solid understanding of at least one Customer IAM product (e.g. Okta, ForgeRock, Azure B2C)
 * Good understanding of Single Sign On, Identity Federation and the related authentication protocols including OAuth, OIDC and SAML.
 * Working knowledge of ITIL processes, particularly Request, Problem, Incident and Change Management, how they interrelate, and how they can be adopted successfully.
 * Basic understanding of IAM governance, risk and compliance principles and processes.
   
   

Certifications/licenses

 * CISSP/ CISM/ CRISC/ ITIL preferred
