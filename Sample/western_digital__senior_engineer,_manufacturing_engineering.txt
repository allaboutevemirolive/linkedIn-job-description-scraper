Job Description

 * Manufacturing Engineering Process Engineer for Solid State Drive (SSD) Backend Assembly Operation, covering the Product customization build from Labeling, inspection and Packing Process in the production line.
 * Define the Product Labeling Process at both Manual and Automation line for SSD Products, with error proof systems interlock.
 * Maintaining the Master Data Attribute for the Labeling and Packaging Material.
 * Create/Generate the Automation Machine recipes/program for Label Placement, Auto Inspection of Final Product (including the information printed on the Product label) and Packing process.
 * Design, validate, and analyze for Product quality which includes the Product cosmetic check as well as the Product label quality (Printing Smear/Alignment, placement alignment and etc.) and continuous improvement on the Backend Assembly processes to maximize the UPH/output.
 * Provide engineering support to project teams to resolve production and quality issues (backend Process).
 * Collaborate and communicate with internal customers directly on Labeling related issue including both Manual process as well as Automated process.
 * Drive for process cpk improvement to meet cpk goal of >1.67 and stretch goal of >2.0.
 * Drive for Process Yield FPY improvement to meet >99.7%.
 * Define process critical parameter CTQ with trigger limit being define, with trigger alarm being turn on. Define process troubleshooting procedure for manufacturing and OCAP.
 * Drive for process automation and data automated reporting for real time process improvement.
 * Leading the Final Inspection Machine calibration and MSA with supplier.
 * Line e-FAI Conversion buy off for all Labeling process - both Manual and Automated Machine.
 * Design & Review automation software solutions for SSD Automated Labeling Process (customize machine)
 * Perform automation software coding standardization / upgrade by to enable SECS/GEM and Database integration alias closely with MES team.
 * Enable automation performance enhancement along with automation engineering team (machine development team).
 * Enable machine performance data availability and automation system integration through collaboration with IT team.
 * Enable APC initiative, Machine learning and Artificial Intelligent programming to enable Light-off Automation manufacturing environment.
 * Receipt Management Systems for each machine.
 * Inspection Program/Reciept optimization
