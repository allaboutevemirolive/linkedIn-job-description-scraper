In This Role, You’ll Get To




 * Provide system troubleshooting support for Tier 2+ cases , and conduct root-cause analysis to identify underlying issues and provide recommendations
 * Develop test scenarios, convert them into test plans, and conduct unit testing
 * Perform basic configuration updates across our HRIS platforms
 * Collaborate closely with the Implementation team to develop and test solution designs
 * Analyze ticket data and trends to identify opportunities for continuous improvement
 * Conduct testing of new feature releases and provide reports on findings
 * Create and maintain documentation for processes and standard operating procedures




What You’ll Need to Succeed




 * Minimum of 2 years of experience as an HRIS support analyst
 * Strong analytical and problem-solving abilities
 * Capable of understanding raised issues, identifying root causes, and collaborating with the team to implement solutions
 * Proficient in formulating test scenarios and creating comprehensive test plans
 * Detail-oriented and curious about system behaviors
 * Proficient in English
 * Demonstrates teamwork, takes ownership, and is a quick learner
 * Able to work well under pressure




It’s Great If You Have




 * Experience in HRIS configuration
 * Familiarity with HRIS platforms like Workday, Greenhouse, Zendesk, learning platforms
 * Exposure to working in an international environment
