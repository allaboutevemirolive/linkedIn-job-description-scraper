Job Description

The Junior Analyst is responsible for processing data and ensuring the data’s accuracy and quality. The incumbent is accountable for data processing tasks from the various regions around the world. 

Key Tasks & Responsibilities

 * Ensures the data integrity during data processing.
 * Verify the accuracy and validity of data processed.
 * Resolve and check any quality issues raised by supervisors/clients.
 * Maintain positive working relationships and open communication with local, regional and global colleagues.
 * Continually innovate, develop and suggest enhancements to gain better work efficiency
 * Ensure that the company’s regional and global standards are adhered to at all times
   

Skills

 * Sound computer skills in areas of MS Word, MS Excel, and MS PowerPoint
 * Speed is of the essence for data processing specialists. Ability to manage time spent on a project is important.
 * Ability to work independently with the ability to prioritize and multi-task; able to think rationally under pressure.
 * Good relationship building skills (internal, external and cross-cultural)
 * Fast leaner and able to adapt quickly
 * Attention to details
 * Patient, flexible and numerate; quality consciousness
 * Ability to coordinate a number of projects at any one time
 * Innovative
   

Personal Attributes

 * Ability to work under the pressure of deadlines and targets
 * Positive ‘can do’ mindset
 * Committed and dedicated
 * Prepared to take ownership of issues and responsibility for actions
 * Strong work ethic and reliability
 * Team player
 * Professionalism and discrete
 * Able to work independently with minimum supervision
 * Self-motivated
   

Specific Qualifications

 * Preferably a Degree/Diploma holder.
 * Good computer skills and broad interest in internet research
 * Interest in consumer product specifications
 * Fresh graduate is encouraged to apply
